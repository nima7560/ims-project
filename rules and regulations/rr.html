Rules and Regulations


1/ Formal and acceptable behaviors from employees.

2/ employees are liable to protect the company’s belongings for office purposes.

3/ employees should preserve official documents for future demand.

4/ The company will not allow employees to work under the influence of alcoholic beverages during work time.

5/ no employee shall drive the company’s vehicles or operate any official equipment under the influence of an alcoholic.

6/ Our Company does not inspire you to drink alcohol or prevent you from drinking alcohol; however, our Company suggests you not drink alcohol when you are on duty.

7/ Every employee has to apply for a special vacation at least one month before the expected date of asking for a vacation.

8/ If any employee wants to leave the job, he/she must submit an official resignation letter 1 month before resignation.
